What are tasks?


Tasks are items on your to-do list. Tasks can be further divided into several sub-tasks. Memoranda will automatically show you deadlines, upcoming tasks, etc on the Agenda panel.


Make sure you update the progress report on each task as you work on it. Memoranda will remind you if tasks are not completed before their end date.
