Deleting a project

Important notes:


Warning: Once deleted, projects are lost permanently and cannot be opened again.


You cannot delete the current project being worked on (the one whose name is in the title bar).



Deleting a project:

  1. Click on the symbol at the top right end of the window. You should see a list of projects like this:

       

       


  1. Click on a project to select it, then press the delete key on your keyboard (Alternatively you may then Right-click and select Delete Project).


The following confirmation box will appear:



  1. Select 'Yes' to delete the project.
